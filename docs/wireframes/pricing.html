Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time. You'll continue to have access until the end of your billing period.

What's the +30 second early access?

We offer a 7-day free trial for Pro and Pro+ plans. If you're not satisfied after the trial, simply cancel before it ends and you won't be charged.
